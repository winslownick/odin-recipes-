Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs with chckien and herbs

Here we have a morning/brunch staple in our world of scrambled eggs with chicken and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 ounces of pre cooked chikcen chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Everything bagel seasoning
  • Pepper
  • Avacado
  • Cherry tomatos

Instructions

  1. Slice your chicken into smaller pieces.
  2. Begin by adding olive oil to the pan and get it medium hot.
  3. Crack two eggs into the pan and cooking until they are yellow and bit brown.
  4. Add chicken in about 1 min before the eggs are done to heat them up.
  5. Put the eggs and chikcen in a bowl and add your avadcado and everything seasoning into the bowl and mix.
  6. Now throw the cherry tomatoes into the bowl and mix in 1 tablespoon of salt and pepper and mix.
  7. Serve it!
