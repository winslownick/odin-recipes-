Mid Rare Steak Recipe

Picture of a medium rare steak

Let's dive into creating a simple medium rare steak that tastes delicious and only takes about 10 mins total to make.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  1. Lay out your steak on a flat surface and seaason both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Marinate the steak with 2 table spoons of worchestershire sauce.
  3. Add olive oil to your pan and heat up your pan until it is very hot and then put your steak into the pan.
  4. Cook on one side for 3 to 4 mins and the other side for 3-4 mins.
  5. Take steak out of the pan and let it simmer for 1 minute before cutting into it.
  6. Enjoy!
