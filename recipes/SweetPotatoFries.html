Sweet Potato Fries

Season Cook Sweet Potato Fries

Get ready to enjoy the crunchy, soft and flavorful sweet potato fries. Pakced with simple ingridients, this will leave you wanting more after they are gone!

Ingridients

  • 2 Sweet Poatatos
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak seasoning
  • Cheyenne Pepper
  1. Grab 2 sweet potatos, a cutting knife and a cutting board.
  2. Chop potatos into the size of how you would like your fries and put them into a mixing bowl.
  3. Add oilve and mix the fries togther so that they are damp with oil.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of salt, pepper, steak seasoning, and cheyenne pepper and mix all together.
  5. Set air fryer to 400 degrees and once heated, place fries in for 15-17 mins.
  6. Shake the fries when it's halfway done cooking.
  7. Enjoy!
